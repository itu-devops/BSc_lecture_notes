## From Monitoring to Logging

Can you answer the following two questions about the performance of your _ITU-MiniTwit_ with your group?

  * Which of your API endpoints is the slowest? How slow is it?
  * Where is the time being spent in this endpoint?
  * Who tries to log into your server(s)?

How do you find out this info?

Note, the project is not about competing who has a faster implementation. However, in the real world you usually want to know performance information because it is very likely that you will look for optimization candidates among your slowest endpoints.
